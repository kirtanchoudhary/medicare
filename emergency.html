Essential Emergency Services

Use these emergency helplines in urgent situations. Contact the appropriate service immediately based on the type of emergency. In critical moments, staying calm and sharing the exact location clearly can help emergency teams respond faster.

Primary Emergency Services

Immediate-response helplines
Police
100

Dial 100 for reporting crimes, theft, or immediate danger.

Fire Brigade
101

Dial 101 for fires, explosions, or gas leaks.

Ambulance
102 108

Dial 102 for free medical transport or 108 for general medical emergencies and accidents.

Specialized Helplines

Support for specific emergencies
Women Helpline
1091 181

Dial 1091 for women in danger or 181 for domestic abuse support.

Child Helpline
1098

Dial 1098 for reporting child abuse or assisting children in distress.

Disaster Management
1078 1077

Dial 1078 or 1077 for natural disasters like floods and earthquakes.

Railway Accident
1072

Dial 1072 for train-related emergencies.

Road Accident
1073 1033

Dial 1073 for road accidents or 1033 for incidents on national highways.
